## Qmark
Question mark pinging game
Ever felt frustrated when your teammate misses a minion, but you couldn't ping in time?
Now you can train your pinging speed!
Introducing!!!! Qmark!!!

## How to play
Download the release.zip
unzip the file and double click Qmark.exe
There are 2 modifiers:

 - Show pinging range (within thise range your ping will register as a point)
 - Minion fading hint (still shows the minion sprite for a few seconds after you have to ping)
